Akasha

DJ Akasha loves deep house mixed with mystic poetry. Ideally he plays at intimate venues for a crowd of 'worldly' people. His sets take you on a journey through the orient. Private sensual parties are his favorite.

ASTRIX

Avi Shmailov under his stage name Astrix, is an Israeli trance music DJ and producer specializing in the subgenre of Progressive psychedelic trance. In 2006 Astrix reached #41 in DJMag's "Top 100 DJs" yearly rank along with a rise of several other Israeli musicians, marking a significant proportion of the Israeli electronic music scene. In 2007, Astrix reached #18 on the list

DJ MAU

Influenced by the american and european underground movement, Mau Mau started his DJ career at the legendary Madame Sata Club, São Paulo 1987. One of the pioneers of the electronic music in Brazil, he was the resident DJ at Sra.Krawitz club and Hell's Club, the club that was responsable for disseminating the electronic music in Brazil.

Dmitri Vegas & Like Mike

Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike are a Greek/Belgian DJ duo composed of brothers Dimitri Thivaios and Michael Thivaios. They were ranked No. 1 in 2015 and 2019, and were ranked No. 2 in 2014, 2016, 2017, 2018 and 2020 on DJ Mag's Top 100 DJs list.

Tiesto

Tijs Michiel Verwest OON, known professionally as Tiësto, is a Dutch DJ and record producer from Breda, North Brabant. He was named "the Greatest DJ of All Time" by Mix magazine in a poll voted by the fans. In 2013, he was voted by DJ Mag readers as the "best DJ of the last 20 years"

Day Din

Day.din aka Deniz Aydin started his musical career not only by kick-drumming with about 4 and a half month before birth with a hard driven 4-to-the-floor beat as his mother keeps telling with her eyes rolling, no, he also joined the house infected party crowd when he was about 16 years old, totally impressed by what he was exposed to on psytrance parties.
